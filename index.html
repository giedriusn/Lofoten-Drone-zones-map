Drone rules — under 250 g

Norway, open category A1. These operational rules apply on top of the zones on the map.

Verified against primary law — EU 2019/947 & 2019/945, the Norwegian forskrift om ubemannede luftfartøyer (BSL A 7-2, FOR-2024-11-01-2777), naturmangfoldloven, and Datatilsynet guidance. A reference, not legal advice — confirm before flying.

① Aircraft, registration & age

  • Register as operator at flydrone.no if your drone has a camera/sensor — required even under 250 g. Only camera-free or certified-toy drones are exempt. Put your operator number on the drone. EU 2019/947 Art 14(5),(8)
  • No exam or course needed for a sub-250 g (C0 / self-built) drone — just know the manual. EU 2019/947 UAS.OPEN.020(4)
  • Min. pilot age 16 — no minimum for a self-built <250 g drone, a toy-class C0, or when flying under supervision of someone 16+. EU 2019/947 Art 9
  • Insurance not legally required under 250 g (open category); ≥250 g needs ≥750 000 SDR third-party cover. Still recommended. BSL A 7-2 §6 · luftfartsloven §11-2 · FOR-2004-07-06-1101 §4

② In the air

  • Max 120 m above the closest point of the ground (the ceiling follows the terrain over hills/valleys). EU 2019/947 UAS.OPEN.010(2) · Art 4(1)(e)
  • Keep it in sight (VLOS) at all times — FPV goggles require a spotter. EU 2019/947 Art 4(1)(d) · UAS.OPEN.060(2)(b)
  • People: A1 lets you overfly individual uninvolved people, but never assemblies / crowds. EU 2019/947 UAS.OPEN.020(2)
  • C0 design limits: <250 g, ≤19 m/s. EU 2019/945 Annex Part 1

③ Where you can't fly (shown on the map)

  • No closer than 5 km to an airport without permission from the air traffic service. BSL A 7-2 §7
  • Not over/near military areas & vessels, embassies or prisons without the local commander's permission. BSL A 7-2 §7
  • Respect control & traffic zones (CTR/TIZ), restricted & danger areas — all the airspace layers.
  • Filming designated military/prohibited areas needs NSM permission. FOR-2018-06-22-951 §6 · lov 2017-06-21-88

④ Wildlife & nature (applies regardless of weight)

  • Don't disturb wildlife — anywhere. "Ved enhver aktivitet skal unødig skade og lidelse på viltlevende dyr og deres reir, bo eller hi unngås." naturmangfoldloven §15 (+ §6)
  • Banned in national parks and most large landscape areas, and in many nature reserves — where a verneforskrift bans "modellfly", that includes drones (take-off, landing and flying in). Miljødirektoratet · nml §77
  • Seabird reserves: seasonal access ban (ferdselsforbud), commonly 15 Apr–31 Jul during nesting — exact dates per area. e.g. Bliksvær FOR-2002-12-06-1426 §3

⑤ Privacy (camera)

  • GDPR / personopplysningsloven applies when you capture identifiable people or property. Purely private use is exempt, but you generally need consent to publish footage of identifiable people, and must never film people in their home/garden without permission. Be visible; inform people nearby. Datatilsynet · GDPR Art 2(2)(c)

Always confirm on flydrone.no / the SafeToFly app and check NOTAMs before flying.
